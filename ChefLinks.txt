The following are some excellent links for use with Chef:
https://learn.chef.io - This is  the high-level link for all the Chef resources.  Very good place to visit.
https://learn.chef.io/tutorials/ - Here’s where to find some excellent Chef tutorials
https://learn.chef.io/skills/ - Here’s a place to find some training resources that go beyond basics skills and into the realm of thought leadership and concepts
https://docs.chef.io - Chef has one of the richest sets of documentation we’ve ever seen.
